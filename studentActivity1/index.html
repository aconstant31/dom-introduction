Mario's Plumbing, Inc.

Mario is everyone's favorite virtual plumber.
In fact, most people prefer him to a plumber in real life.
He can do many things that make him unique from other plumbers, including:

  • Jumping high
  • Hitting blocks
  • Eating mushrooms
  • Collecting coins
  • Touching stars
  • And much more!

The next time you're thinking about calling a plumber, try Mario at 1-800-MY-MARIO!
